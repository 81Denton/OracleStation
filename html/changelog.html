Joan updated:

  • Geis bindings no longer decay faster if not pulled by a Servant, but last for 20 seconds, from 25.
  • Geis bindings will decay slower when on a Sigil of Submission, and, if being pulled by a Servant when crossing a Sigil of Submission, will helpfully remove the pull.

29 July 2017
